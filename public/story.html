Our Story

From DShark to Salt & Shore — a new chapter on Aquarius Beach.

How It All Began

Salt & Shore was born from something real — a beloved restaurant, a loyal community, and a bold new dream. We started as DShark Restaurant, a place that built its name on great food and even greater hospitality right here on the Kenyan coast.

In 2025, we made a decision that felt both exciting and deeply right — to rebrand, relocate, and reimagine. We moved just a splash away to our new home on Aquarius Beach, Watamu, and Salt & Shore was born.

To every guest who supported DShark — this is for you. The best is yet to come.

Aquarius Beach, Watamu

We didn't just move — we found our perfect home. Aquarius Beach in Watamu is one of the most breathtaking stretches of coastline in Kenya, and Salt & Shore sits right at its heart.

The sand, the Indian Ocean, the warm coastal breeze — it's all part of the experience. When you dine with us, the beach isn't just a backdrop. It's the whole point.

What We Believe In

The rebrand wasn't just a name change. It was a commitment — to a broader menu, a stylish boutique atmosphere, and a dining experience made for showing off. We wanted to create something Watamu had never quite seen before.

  • Ocean-to-table freshness
  • Rooted in the Watamu community
  • A broader, bolder menu
  • Boutique atmosphere, made for memories
  • Hospitality that feels like coming home
